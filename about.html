ABOUT CYBERX

CyberX is a fully legal ethical security platform, built for learners who want to explore the world of cybersecurity in a safe environment. Founded by Kamran Liaquat in 2023, it provides an educational hub for ethical hacking and digital security.

The platform simulates real-world security tools and concepts, allowing beginners and intermediate learners to understand networks, systems, and cybersecurity threats without risking real systems.

FEATURES & LEARNING

CyberX provides a modern, terminal-inspired interface with practical modules including:

  • Ethical Hacking Labs
  • Linux Lab Simulations
  • Email Breach & Password Checker
  • Port Scanning & IP Tracking
  • Text Encryption & Decryption Tools
  • Cryptography Labs & Simulations
  • Cybersecurity News & Dark Web Awareness

Each module is beginner-friendly but also valuable for intermediate learners. Users can follow a clear ethical hacking roadmap to gradually improve their cybersecurity skills.

ETHICAL NOTICE

All activities on CyberX are 100% legal. This platform is intended for learning only. Misusing tools in real-world illegal activities is strictly discouraged. The goal is knowledge building and awareness.

By supporting this platform via donation, you help continue developing free and safe learning resources.
