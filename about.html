ABOUT CYBERX

CyberX is a fully legal ethical security platform, built for learners who want to explore cybersecurity in a safe environment. Founded by Kamran Liaquat in 2023.

It provides an educational hub for ethical hacking and digital security, simulating real-world tools and concepts for learners without risking real systems.

FEATURES & LEARNING

  • Ethical Hacking Labs
  • Linux Lab Simulations
  • Email Breach & Password Checker
  • Port Scanning & IP Tracking
  • Text Encryption & Decryption Tools
  • Cryptography Labs & Simulations
  • Cybersecurity News & Dark Web Awareness
