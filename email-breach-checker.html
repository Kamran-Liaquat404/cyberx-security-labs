Immediate Steps if Breached

  1. Change passwords immediately.
  2. Enable MFA / 2FA wherever possible.
  3. Check for reused passwords across accounts.
  4. Monitor bank and important accounts for suspicious activity.
  5. Use a password manager to generate unique credentials.
  6. Consider credit monitoring if personal data was exposed.
