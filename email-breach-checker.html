If an email is breached — Immediate steps

  1. Change passwords immediately on affected services (use strong unique passwords).
  2. Enable MFA / 2FA wherever possible (authenticator apps or hardware keys recommended).
  3. Check for reused passwords — rotate any account using same password.
  4. Monitor bank & important accounts for suspicious activity; inform your bank if needed.
  5. Use a password manager to generate and store unique credentials.
  6. Consider credit monitoring if personal identifying data was exposed.
